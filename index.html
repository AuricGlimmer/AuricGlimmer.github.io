About.

Hello! I'm a 24 year old game designer working on a lot of different projects. I have nearly a decade of game design experience, starting with personal projects for friends and eventually becoming a lead programmer on a game that released a trailer during EVO 2022. Below, you'll find a lot of the games I've worked on in the past and what I'm working on currently - along with some proof of concepts that you can toy with right in your browser!!

Knockout.

A fighting game based in the heart of an iconic animation community.

Knockout is an IKEMEN Fighting game with an extensive history. Started by myself and Dillon Murphy in 2017, Knockout has now been featured at multiple tournaments and events, including making a brief appearance in EVO, the world's largest fighting game event.

I directed and directly programmed a majority of the code before moving on to other projects, and occasionally dipped back in to help as needed.

Roles:

-Lead Programmer

-Co-Director

-Additonal Combat Designer

-Consultant

SCP: Cross Containment.

The SCP Fighting game.

This is an immersive fighting game set within the SCP Universe. Featuring 18 characters from across the universe,SCP: Cross Containment is a product of mnay years of intense development. The game frequently found itself at over 3,000 upvotes on the SCP Subreddit per post, quickly gaining noteriety and attention. This is my largest project to date, and my first time working with a team. I aim to return to this game with more knowledge and experience in the future because I know the potential for this game is still untapped.

Roles:

-Lead Programmer

-Director

-Combat Designer

-Lead Artist

-Character Animator

-Environment Designer
